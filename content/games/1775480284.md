## 18-04-2026

I've been waiting this game since i tried the demo out, what took me back then was the gameplay and the glimpses of the relation between Diana and Hugh. Now i'm trying out the game and what can i say except that Capcom decided to double down with masterpieces after [Resident Evil Requiem](/1773607128.md) by leveraging the RE engine in an absurd way graphic is incredible and Diana hairs looks amazing, also all of modern graphic techs are supported from DLSS to path tracing (unfortunately i couldn't been able to tried out on linux 😧).

I love how the relation between Diana and Hugh unfolds, at first Hugh seems skeptic about being helped by and android controlled from AI but overtime it build trust and it start to act like his father, caring for here and helping here out, i found so cute the mechanic of giving gifts to here and see is reaction
