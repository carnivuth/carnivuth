So Forgive me father 2.....
It's been a wile since i discovered this game steam page, and today i decided to try it, for what i could see seemed more frenetic that what it is in reality. As the standard for this boomer shooter types of games, it's a gameplay driven experience with strange horror vibes, the narrative component it's displayed with collectible objects hidden inside the maps which his something that i don't like much.

The art design is incredible with this hand drawn look and the Cthulhu inspired weapons
