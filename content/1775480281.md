## 26-04-2026

I had some doubts about playing this game, my major concern was the roguelike experience that from previous games i found that it doesn't suits me, personally i don't like the idea of repeating the same sections over and over and create a one shot build. But recently the game has gone on sale on steam for like 6 euros so i decided to try it out and if i found myself to dislike it i could also ask for a refund.

![](/1777217833.png)
> The game just got me i played it non stop for a full day and i already have 10 hours of playtime 🫠

One of the things that i really like about the game is how the story merges with the roguelike experience. The interaction between characters are influenced from the encounters on the various runs, it seems like the characters are stuck with you in the gameplay loop and are growing as you proceed to your journey escaping from hell
