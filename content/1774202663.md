## 22-03-2026

This game was not on my radar but a friend of mine wanted to play it together so here we are...

The atmosphere is tremendous, leaving you with a sense of desolation and hopelessness like any other game i played before. The things that surprised me the most was character design, people in the game have real reactions to what is happening, the game tries to make you trust no one and put you in a situation where you are in the bring of death to test your morals and choices cause what you do when the world could end tomorrow?
