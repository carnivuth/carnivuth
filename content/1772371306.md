## 01-03-2026

The game seems in an early development stage, some assets seems default ones from the unreal engine and the narrative component seems non existent.

Gameplay wise the game reminds me of the good part of destiny with some good monster designs and a good shooting with abilities and perks to make the game less repetitive. The game has a roughlike structure with subsequent stages and perk choices that you can do at the end of the run nothing fancy but it does the job.

For now not an incredible game.
