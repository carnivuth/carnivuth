## 13-04-2026

It's been a while since i bought this game, what can i say about it. It's the first chapter where gliders where introduced also with customizable karts, but **THAT'S NOT IMPORTANT**, the important think is the feeling when  a red shell is coming for you and you can't do a shit about it.
Jokes aside, i bought this game when i started traveling for my new job and for me it's a moment of relax when traveling to work, where i can just avoid thinking for 15 minutes and focus on something meaningless but meaningful at the same time.

> [!NOTE]
> Also i got all gold trophies at 150cc 😎
