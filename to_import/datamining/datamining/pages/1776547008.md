In order to avoid over fitting  pruning can be done

## c4.5 strategy

based on the error, it prunes the leaves if the maximum error of the root of a subtree is lower than the weighted sum of the errors of the leaves

$$eroot <= ei$$

where:

$$
ei = \sum_{leaf}{e}
$$
